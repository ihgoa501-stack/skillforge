# Methodology Registry

A curated, cross-referenced collection of proven methodologies from domain experts. Organized by domain with skill-type applicability markers.

> **Version:** 2.0 | **Entries:** 80+ | **Last updated:** 2026-05

---

## How to Use This Registry

1. **Identify domains** — Map your skill to 1-3 relevant domains below
2. **Select experts** — Choose 1-3 experts whose approach aligns with the goal
3. **Check skill type fit** — Note the "Best for" column to match methodology to skill type
4. **Go to primary sources** — Use the Source column to find original material
5. **Extract actionable principles** — Distill concrete techniques, not just concepts
6. **Resolve conflicts** — When methodologies disagree, choose based on context

## Expanding the Registry

When a domain isn't covered:

1. Web search for "[domain] who is the best practitioner" (NOT "best practices author")
2. Look for: practitioners with verifiable results, respected by peers, showing specific techniques
3. Identify their core approach — what do they DO differently?
4. Cross-reference with existing skill types to mark compatibility
5. Add to the appropriate section with source attribution

**Quality filter for new entries:**
- Primary source available (can verify the methodology directly)?
- Specific, actionable techniques (not just "be good at X")?
- Practitioner has demonstrated results (not just popularity)?
